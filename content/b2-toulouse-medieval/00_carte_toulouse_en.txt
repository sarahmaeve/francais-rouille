Walking Tour Map — Medieval Toulouse


## Walking Tour of the Historic Center

Duration : approximately 2 hours
Distance : 2.5 km
Difficulty : easy, flat route


### Itinerary

1. Starting Point — Place du Capitole
   The heart of Toulouse since the Middle Ages. Meet in front of the Capitole façade, seat of municipal power since the 12th century.

2. Basilique Saint-Sernin
   The largest Romanesque church in Europe. A masterpiece of Romanesque art and a major stop on the Way of Saint James to Compostela.

3. Rue du Taur
   Ancient Roman road linking the Capitole to Saint-Sernin. Its name recalls the martyrdom of Saint Saturninus, dragged by a bull.

4. Église des Jacobins
   A jewel of southern Gothic art. Former Dominican convent, founded to combat the Cathar heresy.

5. Place Saint-Georges
   Former medieval marketplace. Site of executions during the Inquisition.

6. Hôtel de Bernuy
   Magnificent Renaissance mansion, testament to the wealth of the pastel (woad) merchants of the 16th century.
