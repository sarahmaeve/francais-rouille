### About the Novel

In *Les Flammes de l'Oubli*, Kamel Daoud continues his exploration of wounded memories and the silences of history. The novel follows the journey of Selma, an archivist in Algiers who discovers a forgotten manuscript in the basement of the National Library. This mysterious text will upend her understanding of the past and draw her into a quest across three cities and three eras.

*"A powerful and luminous novel that interrogates memory with rare grace."* — Le Monde des Livres
