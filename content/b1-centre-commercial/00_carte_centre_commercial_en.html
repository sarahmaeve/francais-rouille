La Madeleine Shopping Center — Map

Directory of the shopping center in Saint-Malo

Ground Floor (Rez-de-chaussée)

The ground floor is dominated by the Carrefour hypermarket, which takes up about half the level. This is where you'll find groceries, household products, and everyday essentials. Next to it are a Paul bakery (part of the national chain), a tabac-presse (newsagent and tobacconist — also where you buy lottery tickets), the Ruellan traiteur (a prepared-food shop selling high-quality ready-made dishes), and a small sit-down restaurant called Le Comptoir.

First Floor (1er Étage)

The first floor has a mix of specialty shops: Yves Rocher (plant-based cosmetics and skincare), Kiabi (affordable ready-to-wear clothing), Histoire d'Or (jewelry), Alain Afflelou (optician), a pharmacy, SFR (phone and mobile plans), Jean-Louis David (hair salon), and a McDonald's for fast food.

Second Floor (2e Étage)

The second floor is home to Intersport (sporting goods and athletic shoes), Fnac (books, music, and electronics), Maisons du Monde (home décor), Cultura (arts and crafts supplies), and a four-screen cinema.

Parking

There is an underground car park with 800 spaces beneath the center.
