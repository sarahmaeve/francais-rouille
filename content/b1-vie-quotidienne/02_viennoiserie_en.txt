Ordering at a Pastry Shop

Characters:
- Sophie — a customer who enters the bakery in the morning
- Antoine — the baker behind the counter


Antoine : Good morning, ma'am! What would you like this morning?

Sophie : Good morning! Well, I'm hesitating a bit. What do you recommend today?

Antoine : The chocolate pastries just came out of the oven — they're still piping hot. And our apple turnovers are very good too.

Sophie : Mmm, it smells really good. I'll take two chocolate pastries and one apple turnover, please.

Antoine : Excellent choice. Is that for here or to go?

Sophie : To go, please. It's for breakfast at the office.

Antoine : Alright. Would you like anything else? We also have butter croissants and brioches.

Sophie : Are the croissants homemade?

Antoine : Yes, absolutely. Everything is made here, in our workshop, every morning starting at four o'clock.

Sophie : In that case, add three croissants, please. My colleagues are going to be happy.

Antoine : With pleasure. So, two chocolate pastries, one apple turnover, and three croissants. Is that everything?

Sophie : Yes, that's everything. How much does that come to?

Antoine : That comes to eight euros fifty, please.

Sophie : Here you go, I'm paying by card. Does contactless work?

Antoine : Yes, of course. Place your card here. And there you go, all done! Thank you and have a good day, ma'am!

Sophie : Thank you! See you tomorrow, probably. Have a good day!
