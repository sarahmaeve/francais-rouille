An Evening with Kamel Daoud

On the occasion of the publication of his new novel
« Les Flammes de l'Oubli »
(The Flames of Forgetting)

Éditions Gallimard is pleased to invite you to an exceptional evening with Kamel Daoud, winner of the 2024 Prix Goncourt, on the occasion of the publication of his new novel Les Flammes de l'Oubli, published in the prestigious Collection Blanche.

Evening Program

  • 8:30 PM Audience questions
  • 8:50 PM Book signing
  • 9:30 PM End of the evening

About the Novel

In Les Flammes de l'Oubli, Kamel Daoud continues his exploration of wounded memories and the silences of history. The novel follows the journey of Selma, an archivist in Algiers who discovers a forgotten manuscript in the basement of the National Library. This mysterious text will upend her understanding of the past and draw her into a quest across three cities and three eras.

"A powerful and luminous novel that interrogates memory with rare grace."

— Le Monde des Livres
