A Visit to the Bakery — The House Specialties

B1 — English Translation

Characters

  • Claire — a curious customer who enters the bakery
  • Monsieur Duval — the owner and pastry chef of the bakery
Claire: Good morning, sir! Your display window is beautiful. Everything looks delicious.
Monsieur Duval: Good morning, ma'am! Thank you, that's kind. What tempts you today?
Claire: That's just it — I'm having a hard time choosing. What do you recommend? I'm looking for something special.
Monsieur Duval: Well, if you like chocolate, I recommend our dark chocolate éclair. It's our best seller.
Claire: A dark chocolate éclair? What's special about it?
Monsieur Duval: We use seventy-two percent dark chocolate, imported directly from a Belgian artisan. The pastry cream is made fresh every morning, and the glaze is prepared to order.
Claire: That sounds incredible. Is there anything else you recommend?
Monsieur Duval: Yes, absolutely. Our house specialty is the Paris-Noisette. It's an original creation by my wife.
Claire: The Paris-Noisette? I don't know it at all. What is it exactly?
Monsieur Duval: It's a crispy puff pastry with a Piedmont hazelnut mousse and a salted butter caramel center. We sprinkle it with toasted hazelnuts and a touch of fleur de sel.
Claire: Oh my, that sounds extraordinary. Your wife invented this recipe?
Monsieur Duval: Yes, she's a pastry chef too. She worked for ten years at Ladurée before we opened our own shop. The Paris-Noisette is her pride and joy.
Claire: Well then, I'll take both! One dark chocolate éclair and one Paris-Noisette, please.
Monsieur Duval: Excellent choice! The éclair is three euros fifty, and the Paris-Noisette is five euros. That comes to eight euros fifty altogether.
Claire: Perfect. Do you have a loyalty card?
Monsieur Duval: Yes, of course. On your tenth purchase, you receive a free pastry. I'll prepare one for you right away.
Claire: Great! Thank you very much, sir. I'm sure I'll be back very soon.
Monsieur Duval: I certainly hope so! Enjoy, ma'am. And if you like the Paris-Noisette, tell your friends!
