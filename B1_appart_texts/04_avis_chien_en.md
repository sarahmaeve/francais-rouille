# Notice to Residents — Dogs on Leash

> **NOTICE TO RESIDENTS**
>
> **REMINDER OF BUILDING RULES**
>
> All residents of the building are reminded that dogs must be kept on a leash in all common areas: entrance hall, stairways, corridors, and inner courtyard.
>
> Furthermore, dogs are not permitted on the green spaces of the residence. The lawn in the inner courtyard is a shared space that must remain clean for everyone.
>
> Dog owners are also responsible for the immediate cleanup of their animal's droppings.
>
> Any failure to comply with these rules may result in a formal warning from the building management.
>
> Thank you for your understanding and your respect for the neighborhood.
>
> **The Building Management Association**
> Résidence Les Tables Claudiennes
