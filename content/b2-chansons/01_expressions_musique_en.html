Talking About Music For Dummies

Essential expressions for sharing your opinion about music

This page is a reference guide to the slang and expressions French speakers use when discussing music. The format parodies the popular "Pour Les Nuls" book series (the French equivalent of "For Dummies").

Saying You Like Something

ExpressionMeaningExample
C'est un tube It's a hit "Dernière Danse" is a worldwide hit.
Ça cartonne It's a smash / It's killing it Her latest album is killing it right now.
Ça donne des frissons It gives you chills / goosebumps Her voice gives me chills every time.
C'est une pépite It's a gem / a hidden treasure That hidden track is a real gem.
Je suis accro I'm hooked / addicted I'm hooked on her new album.
Ça envoie du lourd It's heavy / impressive / it slaps The concert was insane — it was heavy.
C'est un chef-d'œuvre It's a masterpiece This album is a masterpiece, period.

Saying You Don't Like Something

ExpressionMeaningExample
C'est quelconque It's mediocre / unremarkable The new songs are pretty unremarkable.
Ça tourne en rond It goes in circles / it's repetitive By the third album, it's going in circles.
C'est formaté It's formulaic / cookie-cutter It's too formulaic — sounds like elevator music.
La prod est trop lisse The production is too slick / overproduced I liked it better when the production was less polished.
J'ai décroché I tuned out / lost interest I tuned out after the second single.
C'est pas à la hauteur It doesn't live up (to expectations) The live show didn't live up to the studio version.

Qualifying Your Opinion

ExpressionMeaningExample
Je suis mitigé(e) I'm mixed / I have mixed feelings I'm mixed on this track — the chorus is good but the verses are weak.
C'est inégal It's uneven / inconsistent The album is uneven: three great tracks, the rest is forgettable.
Je te l'accorde I'll give you that / fair point It's a bit long, I'll give you that, but the ending makes up for it.
C'est un choix artistique It's an artistic choice The lo-fi sound is an artistic choice, not a flaw.
Ça se laisse écouter It's listenable / pleasant enough Her EP is pleasant enough, but nothing to write home about.
