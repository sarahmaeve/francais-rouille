Looking for Running Shoes

B1 — English Translation

Characters

  • Alice — an American tourist shopping at the La Madeleine shopping center
  • Paul — Alice's husband
  • Saleswoman — a saleswoman in a sports shop
Alice: Paul, you need running shoes, right? We should take advantage while we're here.
Paul: Yeah, mine are completely worn out. But is there a sports shop in this mall?
Alice: I don't know, let's look at the directory. Look, there's a sign over there.
Paul: So… Carrefour downstairs, clothing shops on the first floor… Ah, there's an Intersport on the second floor.
Alice: Intersport, that's what we need. Shall we take the escalator?
Paul: Yes, let's go. What's it next to?
Alice: According to the map, it's on the second floor, next to Fnac.
Paul: OK, I see it. Let's go in.
Saleswoman: Hello! Can I help you?
Paul: Hi. I'm looking for running shoes. I jog three times a week.
Saleswoman: Alright. What shoe size are you?
Paul: Uh… in French sizes, I'm not sure. In the US, I'm a 10.
Saleswoman: That's roughly a 43 or 44. We'll try both. What surface do you run on? Roads or trails?
Paul: Mostly roads. Sometimes on dirt paths, but that's rare.
Saleswoman: Then I'd recommend a model with good cushioning. Look, we have these ones on sale. They were ninety euros, now they're sixty-five.
Paul: Oh, that's a good deal. Can I try them in a 43?
Saleswoman: Of course, I'll go get them from the back. Have a seat — I'll be just a minute.
Alice: They're not bad, those ones. Do you like the color?
Paul: I don't care about the color, as long as they're comfortable.
Saleswoman: Here you go, the 43. Try them on both feet — it's important.
Paul: They're comfortable. But they're a bit tight around the toes.
Saleswoman: I'll get the 44 then. That happens a lot with this brand — they run a bit small.
Paul: Thanks.
Saleswoman: Here's the 44. Try these.
Paul: Oh yeah, much better. They fit really well. Alice, what do you think?
Alice: They're great. And at sixty-five euros, we're not going to find better.
Paul: Alright, I'll take them. Do you accept card?
Saleswoman: Yes, of course. The register is this way.
