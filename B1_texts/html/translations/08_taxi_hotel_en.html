Booking a Taxi at the Hotel

B1 Dialogue — English Translation — Gare du Nord to CDG Airport

Characters

  • David — a traveler staying at a hotel near the Gare du Nord
  • Émilie — the hotel receptionist
David: Good evening, sorry to bother you. I need to book a taxi for very early tomorrow morning.
Émilie: Good evening, sir. No problem — that's what I'm here for. Where are you going?
David: To Charles de Gaulle airport. My flight is at nine o'clock, so I'd like to leave at five thirty.
Émilie: Five thirty, okay. That's a good idea to leave early. At that time, there isn't much traffic, but it's better to allow some extra time.
David: That's what I was thinking — how long does the trip usually take?
Émilie: From our area, near the Gare du Nord, you should expect between thirty and forty-five minutes to the airport. It depends on the terminal.
David: I'm at Terminal 2E. That's the farthest one, right?
Émilie: Yes, Terminal 2E is a bit farther, but at five thirty the roads are clear. You should arrive in about thirty-five minutes.
David: And for the price, roughly how much does it cost?
Émilie: The fare between Paris and CDG is a fixed flat rate. It's fifty-three euros from the Right Bank. Since we're in the 10th arrondissement, that's the rate that applies.
David: Okay, fifty-three euros. Is it the same price for all taxis?
Émilie: Yes, it's a regulated fare. All official Parisian taxis must charge this flat rate. No surprises.
David: Very good, that's reassuring. And how do I pay?
Émilie: You can pay by bank card directly in the taxi. Most drivers also accept contactless payment. You can also pay in cash if you prefer.
David: I'll pay by card — it's simpler. And the taxi will pick me up here, in front of the hotel?
Émilie: Exactly. The driver will be at the main entrance at five thirty. I'll book with our partner company — they're always punctual.
David: Should I give a tip?
Émilie: It's not mandatory in France, but it's appreciated. Generally, people round up to the nearest euro or add two or three euros.
David: Perfect, thank you for all this information. Can you confirm the booking?
Émilie: Done, sir. Taxi booked for tomorrow, five thirty, in front of the hotel, destination CDG Terminal 2E. Have a good trip!
