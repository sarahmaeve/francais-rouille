The Prefecture Automated Line

Characters:
- The automated line — the recorded voice of the Loire-Atlantique prefecture

The automated line: Welcome to the voice server of the Loire-Atlantique prefecture. Our reception services are open Monday to Friday, from nine a.m. to half past twelve, and from half past one to four p.m. The prefecture is closed on Wednesday afternoons, as well as on public holidays. Please note that all residence-permit procedures are now handled exclusively online, on the French digital-administration website, at administration-etrangers-en-france.interieur.gouv.fr. No file is accepted at the counter without a prior appointment confirmed by email. To access your request, please dial one of the following options. Press one for civil-status, national ID cards and passports. Press two for driving licences and vehicle registration certificates. Press three for residence permits and naturalisation. Press four to speak to a reception agent, between nine a.m. and eleven a.m., only on Tuesdays and Thursdays. In case of an administrative emergency, please visit our website, emergencies section, or contact your town hall of residence. To listen to this message again, press zero. To return to the main menu at any time, press star. We wish you a good day.
