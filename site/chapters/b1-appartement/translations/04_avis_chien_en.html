Notice to Residents

Reminder of Building Rules

All residents of the building are reminded that dogs must be kept on a leash in all common areas: entrance hall, stairways, corridors, and inner courtyard.

Furthermore, dogs are not permitted on the green spaces of the residence. The courtyard lawn is a shared space and must remain clean for everyone.

Dog owners are also responsible for the immediate cleanup of their animal's droppings.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in a formal warning from the building management.

Thank you for your understanding and respect for the neighborhood.

The Building Management Association
Résidence Les Tables Claudiennes
