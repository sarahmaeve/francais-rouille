Ms. Mercier: Good afternoon, Ms. Moreau, this is Ms. Mercier, Louis's teacher in CM2 at Jules Ferry school. I'm just leaving you a short message — it's ten past five on Tuesday. Nothing serious, don't worry. It's just that there was a small incident at afternoon break between Louis and two of his classmates. I'd like us to talk about it calmly, the three of us, tomorrow morning. If you're available, I'd suggest you drop into the classroom at morning drop-off, between twenty past eight and half past. Otherwise, you can reach me at the school office, on zero three, eighty-seven, twenty-six, forty-four, twelve, during class hours. If neither really suits you, we'll find another slot. Have a good evening, Ms. Moreau. Until tomorrow.
